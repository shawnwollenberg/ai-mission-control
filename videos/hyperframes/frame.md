# Mission Control film system

Dark executive command surface. The focal layer is always a genuine product screenshot or one concise category statement. Teal identifies organizational activity and brand continuity; amber appears only when risk or human judgment enters the story.

Use screenshots as cinematic evidence rather than web pages: crop toward the meaningful region, add restrained depth, and move the virtual camera slowly. Avoid generic card grids, excessive glow, fake dashboards, and motion that does not clarify a real organizational transition.

Headline register: institutional confidence. Supporting detail: technical precision. The film should feel closer to a controlled mission briefing than a SaaS advertisement.
